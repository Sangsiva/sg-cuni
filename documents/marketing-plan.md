# SG CorpUni — Marketing Plan

## Phase 1: Free — Do This Week

### 1. Google Business Profile
- Create at [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
- Gets you on **Google Maps** + the **local business panel**
- List as **"Uniform Supplier"** in Singapore
- Add your website, phone number, and photos

### 2. WhatsApp Business Profile
- Switch to the **WhatsApp Business** app
- Add your catalog, business hours, and auto-reply
- Every WhatsApp chat becomes a potential lead

### 3. Post on LinkedIn
Write 2-3 posts targeting Singapore HR/admin managers:
- *"5 things to check before ordering corporate uniforms in Singapore"*
- *"How much do custom polo shirts actually cost in Singapore?"* (use your pricing data)
- Share your production photos — *"Behind the scenes of how your uniforms are made"*

### 4. Join Facebook Groups
- Search for **"Singapore HR"**, **"Singapore business owners"**, **"Singapore startups"**
- Don't spam — answer questions about uniforms and mention your site naturally

### 5. List on Free Directories
- [sgbusinessdirectory.com](https://sgbusinessdirectory.com)
- [yellowpages.sg](https://yellowpages.sg)
- [yelp.com.sg](https://yelp.com.sg)

## Phase 2: Low Cost — This Month

### 6. Google Ads
- Even **$5-10/day** targeting keywords can drive qualified leads
- Target keywords:
  - "corporate uniforms Singapore"
  - "custom polo shirts Singapore"
- These are **high-intent searches** — people actively looking for what you sell

### 7. Write Blog Posts Targeting Search Terms
- *"Best corporate uniform supplier Singapore 2026"*
- *"How to order custom t-shirts in Singapore"*
- *"Corporate polo shirt vs t-shirt: which is right for your team?"*

### 8. Ask for Google Reviews
- When you get your first Singapore customers, ask them to leave a Google review
- Even **3-5 reviews with photos** massively boosts local search ranking

---

## Phase 3: Smart Outreach

### 9. Cold Email / LinkedIn Message HR Managers
Singapore has ~270,000 registered companies. Target:
- **F&B chains** — need aprons, uniforms
- **Hotels & hospitality**
- **Schools & tuition centres**
- **Tech startups** — team tees, hackathon shirts
- **Sports clubs and running groups**

### 10. Offer a "First Order" Deal
- E.g., *"First 50 pieces at cost price"* or *"Free sample pack for new customers"*
- The Free Sample CTA on the site is already doing this
