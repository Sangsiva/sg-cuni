## Our Competitive Position (SG Corp Uniforms)

**Advantage:** Tirupur manufacturing = lower production costs, especially for embroidery.
**Disadvantage:** Shipping from India adds cost + 2-4 week lead time vs local 5-7 day delivery.

**Strategy:** Price slightly below mid-range for embroidered polos (our strength). Match market on basic tees. Compete on quality + free sample + design mockup.
